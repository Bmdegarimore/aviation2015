Welcome to the Aviation website management page! To unlock a section for students, please click the lock icon so that it is set to an open lock. To configure a section, please click the "make changes" button within the section.

You may also view student results on quiz sections by clicking the progress icon within the section. This will give you the terms that students missed on the test. If you click the report icon, it will present a grade table for the student roster, containing scores and missed terms.
